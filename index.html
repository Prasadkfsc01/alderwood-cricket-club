Our Story

More Than a Cricket Club

Founded in 2022, Alderwood Cricket Club has been at the heart of local cricket for over seven decades. From our ground on Meadow Lane, we've nurtured generations of cricketers, from juniors picking up a bat for the first time to seasoned players competing across the Surrey League.

We field three Saturday XIs, a Sunday social side, a thriving Junior Academy for ages 8–16, and a Ladies' team formed in 2019. Whether you're a competitive cricketer or simply love the game, there's a place for you at Alderwood.

Our pavilion and bar are open to members and guests throughout the season. Come and watch a match on a summer afternoon — there's nothing quite like it.

Our Squads

Club Teams

1st XI

Captain: James Hartley · Surrey Division 2

Our flagship side competing in the Surrey League. Matches every Saturday from late April through to September, with warm-up nets on Thursday evenings.

2nd XI

Captain: Rob Finch · Surrey Division 4

A strong competitive side providing opportunities for developing players and experienced members who prefer a less intensive schedule.

3rd XI

Captain: Dave Patel · Friendly

Friendly fixtures and cup competitions. Welcoming to returning players and those new to competitive cricket looking to gain match experience.

Ladies' XI

Captain: Sarah Okafor · Surrey Women's League

Founded in 2019, our ladies' team has grown rapidly. New players of all abilities welcome — training Tuesday evenings at the ground.

Sunday XI

Captain: Mike Tanner · Social

Relaxed Sunday afternoon fixtures against local clubs. Perfect for those with Saturday commitments who still want to play regular cricket.

Junior Academy

Head Coach: Tom Ellis · Ages 8–16

ECB-qualified coaching every Sunday morning during the season and over winter. Over 60 juniors enrolled across our Under-10, U13, and U16 sides.
